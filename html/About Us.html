KAMSIIE ,formerly Onacouture is an African Fashion clothier brand,which is the brain child of Imoh Kamsiie Ada founded in the spring of 2106. KAMSIIE (Onacouture),is a fashion label that specializes in clean cut bespoke and Pret-a-porter fashion for women,created from the scratch on the principles of fashion,style and creativity.

This brand started in spring of 2017,with private sales within Nigeria. Its aims at breaking boundaries to become an international fashion brand.

KAMSIIE,launched a male line called “MY MAN” in August 2017,exclusively for shirt and pants to give Our today’s Man,an exquisite look, and also Styling for both “male and female” hence the name change to House all the brand under it.

KAMSIIE Iis a one stop brand for everything,from Casual,cocktails,evening, brides and bridesmaids,office and also shirt and pants for guys. 5% of all KAMSIIE are given to chAritable organization,all fabrics are African inspired fabrics and outfits are made in Nigeria.
